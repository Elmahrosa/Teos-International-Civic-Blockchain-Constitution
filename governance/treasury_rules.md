# Treasury Rules

## Vault Creation
- Must be declared in `vault_registry.json`  
- Requires dual verification links  
- Must be logged in `chapter_resurrection.md`

## Asset Injection
- Only Founders may inject assets  
- All injections logged with timestamp and badge ID  
- Example: ERT injection by Elmahrosa Founder

## Transparency
- All flows audited in `audits/`  
- No hidden or off-chain actions allowed  
- Vault status must be "active" to transact
