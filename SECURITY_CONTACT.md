What to Include
To help us triage and fix the issue quickly, please include:

Description: A clear technical description of the vulnerability.
Impact: An assessment of the impact on sovereign integrity, data confidentiality, or system availability.
Proof of Concept: Steps to reproduce the issue (if applicable).
Mitigation: Any suggested workaround or fix (if known).
Response Timeline
We aim to acknowledge receipt within 48 hours and provide a detailed response regarding the next steps within 7 business days.

Coordination
We will coordinate a fix and release plan with you before any public disclosure. We value transparency and will credit the reporter in the security advisory unless you request anonymity.

Preferred Language: English / Arabic
