# Preliminary Access Inquiry (PAI)
## Ministerial Executive Summary

**Issued by:** Elmahrosa International  
**System:** TEOS / ICBC Sovereign Infrastructure  
**Classification:** Public Governance Specification  

---

## Purpose

The **PAI Receipt** is a cryptographic acknowledgment issued **before**
any technical access or execution rights are granted.

It confirms that a ministry or sovereign authority has formally reviewed
and accepted:

- The International Civic Blockchain Constitution (ICBC)
- The TEOS Sovereign License (TESL)

---

## What the PAI Receipt Does

✔ Confirms institutional acknowledgement  
✔ Creates an immutable audit trail  
✔ Prevents silent or informal onboarding  
✔ Protects national sovereignty  
✔ Enables transparent oversight  

---

## What the PAI Receipt Does NOT Do

✘ It does not authorize execution  
✘ It does not move funds  
✘ It does not grant automation rights  

Execution requires a **separate Authorization Stamp**.

- Cryptographically signed
- Timestamped and immutable
- Auditable on-chain
- Governed by constitutional law
- Non-repudiable
