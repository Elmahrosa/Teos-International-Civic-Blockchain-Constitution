# Invitation Manifesto

We invite nations to join under one transparent, petition-first framework.

### Entry Ritual
1. Sign the civic petition:  
   [Sign With It — Egypt Crypto Law Petition](https://www.change.org/p/join-the-movement-sign-the-petition-to-regulate-digital-currencies-in-egypt)
2. Receive badge assignment.  
3. Register vault in `vault_registry.json`.  
4. Log chapter entry in `chapter_resurrection.md`.

### Commitment
- Leaders must log a Founder’s Declaration.  
- Vaults must be dual-verified.  
- Compliance templates must be completed before activation.
