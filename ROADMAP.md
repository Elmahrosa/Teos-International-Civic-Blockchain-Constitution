# Roadmap — International Civic Blockchain Constitution

## Month 0–1: Foundation
- Add README, constitution, land schema, example assets.
- Publish minimal reference contract (AssetRegistry) and examples.
- Create governance & security docs.

## Month 1–3: Tooling & Integrations
- Build CLI tools (anchor, verify, migrate).
- Integrate with IPFS/IPNS & private IPFS for PII.
- Build OpenAPI spec for registries.

## Month 3–6: Pilots & Legal Adapters
- Pilot with 1–2 jurisdiction partners (permissioned chain).
- Build legal adapter templates for mapping to cadastral law.
- Privacy Impact Assessments and DPIA templates.

## Month 6–12: Hardening & Community
- Audits for smart contracts and CLI.
- Establish adopter registry and onboarding playbook.
- Expand asset schemas (procurement, environmental assets, cultural heritage).

## Ongoing
- Maintain RFC process, RFC library.
- Community governance: on-chain upgrade patterns and staging.
- Global outreach and interoperability working groups.
